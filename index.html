Knightyme Danes

We have quality, healthy Great Danes with sound temperaments and lovable personalities!! We are Knightyme Danes and we are located in Sellersburg, Indiana, just 9 miles North of Louisville, Kentucky. We have bred and raised Great Danes for over 18 years. Our breeding program consists of European and American bloodlines. All of our Danes have AKC papers. We breed Beautiful Harlequins, Mantles, Merles, Blues, Chocolates, Blacks, Brindles, Fawns, & Lilac Great Danes. We also have Porcelain, Choclequin & Lilacquin Great Danes. Porcelain Danes have Blue patches, Choclequin Danes have Chocolate patches and Lilaquins have Lavender/purple colored patches.
